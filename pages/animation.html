Season 1 Release date: April 01, 1997

Pokémon: Indigo League

Ash Ketchum begins his journey to become a Pokémon Trainer with an unexpected partner, Pikachu. Alongside friends Brock and Misty, he faces tough Gym Battles and befriends Pokémon like Bulbasaur, Squirtle, and Charmander on his quest to be a Pokémon Master.

Season 2 Release date: January 28, 1999

Pokemon: Adventures in the Orange Islands

Ash competes in the Indigo League, where his friendship with Richie may affect his chances. After finishing his Kanto journey, he travels to the Orange Islands, where Brock stays with Professor Ivy. Now joined by Misty and Pokémon watcher Tracey Sketchit.

Season 3 Release date: October 14, 1999

Pokemon: The Johto Journeys

Ash faces the Orange Crew and their leader, Drake, before returning to Pallet Town, where he reunites with Brock. Together, they head to the Johto region, where Ash begins challenging Gym Leaders, catching new Pokémon like Totodile and Chikorita.
